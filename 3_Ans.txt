Benefits of Using Meta Tags are :-

1. Improved SEO: Meta tags, such as meta titles and meta descriptions, help search engines understand
the content of web pages, leading to better search engine rankings and increased visibility in search results.

2.Enhanced User Experience: Well-crafted meta descriptions can entice users to click on your site in search 
results, increasing click-through rates and attracting more visitors.

3.Viewport control : The viewport meta tag is crucial for responsive web design

4.Structured Information: Meta tags like Open Graph and Twitter Cards provide structured data that social
media platforms use to display rich previews of your web content, making it more appealing when shared
on social networks.
